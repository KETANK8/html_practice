Table Tennis Star Achanta Sharath Kamal To Receive Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award

Table tennis legend Achanta Sharath Kamal will receive the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award, the country's highest sporting honour, from President Droupadi Murmu on November 30 at the Rashtrapati Bhavan in New Delhi. While Sharath is the lone Khel Ratna award recipient, a total of 25 athletes including shuttlers Lakshya Sen, HS Prannoy, woman boxer Nikhat Zareen, athletes Eldhose Paul, Avinash Sable have been nominated for the Arjuna Award this year.

The awardees will receive their awards from the President at a specially organised function at Rashtrapati Bhavan.

Google may soon allow users to convert ChromeOS screen recording into gifs

According to a report by 9to5Google, the ChromeOS team is exploring the possibility of adding support for GIF recordings. The GIF-animated image format has been around for a long time now and is easier to share as they are supported by almost all apps and devices.

The only disadvantage of using GIF, instead of WebM to save screen recordings on ChromeOS will be the size of the file (GIF creates larger files). However, for short recordings, this could be an appropriate tradeoff for convenience.
